## Summary

`0.6.0-rc.2` prepares the post-rc1 correction and roadmap-probe line for the
`next` channel.

The product center remains gate-first:

- workflow/check/finish/archive gates;
- PH-generated evidence and explicit verification records;
- deterministic conformance blockers;
- read-only evidence and P-minus decision-support reports;
- default-off continuation previews.

This prep does not claim token saving, provider-token saving, product efficacy,
navigation benefit, app quality, full-TDD sufficiency, broad reliability,
closure guarantee, autonomous completion, generated-app certification,
deterministic role enforcement, production-ready delegation, automatic
completion/downgrade/removal, or CodeGraph/LSP default/effectiveness.

## Runtime Injection

`runtimeInjection` remains a parked opt-in preview. There is no default change
or removal claim in this release prep.

## Ralph-loop

Ralph-loop remains default-off.

Accepted prior measurement supports hybrid tool-output trigger-survival for the
calibrated fixture, including marker/state/follow-up survival. It does not
prove completion-integrity movement, default-change, autonomous completion,
closure success, or broad reliability.

The blocker-depth update makes continuation wording name the current blocker in
the visible blocker set. That is preparation for better completion-integrity
fixtures, not evidence that completion improved.

## Role Checklist Relay

The public framing is `Role Checklist Relay`.

`--multi-agent-preview` and `multiAgent` remain compatibility flag/config
names. The relay may ask a host to invoke a role subagent when a host task
surface is available, but PH relay remains checklist-first and does not
guarantee automatic OpenCode subagent orchestration or production-ready
delegation.

## Role Boundary

`workflow role-boundary` remains report-only and heuristic.

Runtime write findings are time-window/path/current-role observations. They are
not deterministic role enforcement, closure blockers, blocked writes, auto-fix
actions, or proof that a specific actor wrote a file.

## Registry NO-GO Notes
