## Fix Discovered By This Smoke

The initial `ph workflow finish implement` remained blocked even after OpenCode reran final verification through `npx ph bearshell`.

Root cause:

- the classifier combined a raw-shell retrospective line with separate `bootRun` / `curl` evidence lines;
- this made a report that said "initial raw smoke, final bearshell rerun" look like active raw final verification.

Fix:

- raw final verification is now detected on the same line or as an explicit "raw shell used for final verification" statement;
- a line that states final verification was rerun through bearshell is not blocking.

## Limitations

- This is one clean run.
- It verifies workflow behavior, not generated application product quality.
- It does not prove future agents will always avoid direct `.persona/rules` reads.
- It does not add backend-shape rules or enforcement gates.

## Decision

Proceed with v0.3.1 alpha packaging after full repo verification.

Do not add new backend shape rules from this smoke.

Keep `.persona/rules` direct reads as WARN-only unless a later smoke shows repeated direct reads.
